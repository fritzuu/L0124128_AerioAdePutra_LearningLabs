About Liverpool FC

Liverpool Football Club, founded in 1892, is one of the most successful football clubs in the world. With a rich history and a passionate fanbase, Liverpool FC has won numerous domestic and international titles, including the prestigious UEFA Champions League.

Key Achievements:

  • 6 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 19 English League titles
  • 7 FA Cups
  • 15 FA Community Shields
